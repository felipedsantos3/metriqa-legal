Privacy Policy

Metriqa respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Metriqa mobile application.

Information We Collect

Metriqa allows users to voluntarily enter personal and health-related information, which may include name, age, weight, height, medication details, health logs, and related notes.

How Information Is Used

All information entered into Metriqa is used solely to provide app functionality, such as tracking, visualization, reminders, and summaries for the user.

Data Storage

User data is stored locally on the user’s device. Metriqa does not transmit, store, or process personal data on external servers controlled by the developer.

Data Sharing

Metriqa does not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties.

Analytics and Tracking

Metriqa does not use third-party tracking technologies for advertising purposes. Any analytics, if present, are used only to improve app performance and stability.

Notifications

Metriqa may send local notifications for reminders and alerts based on user preferences. Notifications are generated locally on the device.

User Control

Users maintain full control over their data and may edit or delete information directly within the app at any time.

Children’s Privacy

Metriqa is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
